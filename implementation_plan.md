# SEO Strategy & Implementation Plan: Roof Moss Treatment Victoria

This plan outlines the strategy to transition **Roof Labs Canada** into a high-ranking, SEO-friendly static website targeting the Victoria, BC (Vancouver Island) market.

## User Review Required

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Domain Selection**: Choosing a keyword-rich domain is the single most effective "shortcut" to ranking locally. Please review the suggested names below.
> **Organic Certification**: Can we claim "100% Organic" or "Eco-Safe"? This is a major differentiator against competitors like Thomson.
> **Service Area**: Are you focusing strictly on Greater Victoria, or do you want to target the entire Vancouver Island?

## Phase 1: Branding & Domain Strategy (SEO "Power Move")

Current Name: **Roof Labs Canada**
Analysis: Professional, but "Labs" feels clinical. For local services, descriptive names rank faster.

### Suggested SEO-Friendly Domains
1. **victoriaroofcleaning.ca** (Strongest local relevance)
2. **mossremovalvictoria.ca** (Exact match for the primary search term)
3. **organicroofcarevictoria.ca** (Highlights the "Organic" USP)
4. **islandroofcare.ca** (Good for future expansion)

**Recommendation**: Use a `.ca` domain to signal local relevance to Google Canada.

## Phase 3: Technical Implementation (Static Site)

We will build a high-performance static website. Speed is a critical SEO ranking factor.

### Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Vite + React (or Vanilla HTML/JS for ultra-lightweight).
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS with a "Premium/Clean" aesthetic.
- **Optimization**:
    - **Image Compression**: Next-gen formats (WebP/AVIF) for roof photos.
    - **Semantic HTML**: Proper H1-H6 hierarchy for Google crawlers.
    - **Schema Markup**: LocalBusiness JSON-LD to help Google display your address, reviews, and hours in the "Map Pack".

### Site Map [NEW]
- **index.html**: Hero (Organic focus), Trust signals (5-star reviews), Call to Action (Free Quote).
- **services.html**: Detail on the organic process vs. chemical/pressure wash.
- **about.html**: Story of the startup, commitment to Victoria's environment.
- **contact.html**: High-conversion lead form.
- **locations/**: Sub-pages for each municipality.
